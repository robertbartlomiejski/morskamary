# Licensing and Institutional Compliance

## Data Governance
- **Open Access:** Prioritize CC-BY or similar open licenses for literature extraction.
- **Restricted Data:** Any data from proprietary sources must be flagged in `DATA_GOVERNANCE.txt` and excluded from public exports.
- **GDPR/Privacy:** No personal data (PII) should be stored in the repository. Author names are permitted for citation purposes only.

## Enforcement (Future Stages)
- Stage 4 will introduce automated CI blocks for license mismatches.
- Currently, compliance is a **Human Contract** enforced by the Lead Agent and contributors.
