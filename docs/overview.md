# morskamary: Research Infrastructure Overview

## Mission
To provide a reproducible, auditable, and theoretically grounded infrastructure for **Blue Sociology** and **Blue Economy Competence Mapping**.

## Core Framework: QMBD (Quadripartite Model of Blue Dynamics)
The repository operates on a 4x4x3 Framework:
1. **Marine (M):** Biophysical agency, ecosystems, marinization.
2. **Maritime (T):** Techno-economic mediation, institutions, maritimization.
3. **Oceanic (O):** Planetary-scale governance, oceanization.
4. **Hydronization (H):** Posthuman wet agentism, liquid materialism, water body personhood, more-than-human hybrids, and hydro-social dynamics.

### Domains
Economy, Technology, Politics, Culture.

### Dynamics
Demarinization, Remarinization, Comarinization (applied recursively across all axes).

## Operational Constraints
- **Evidence First:** No data without a DOI or verified source.
- **Portable:** No machine-specific paths in core logic.
- **Staged Evolution:** Changes follow the 5-stage upgrade plan to prevent architectural drift.
