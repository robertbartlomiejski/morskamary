Occupation	Boat Builder
Description of the occupation	The occupation of a BOAT BUILDER is to design, plan and construct recreational boats and large yachts from a variety of materials. This occupation can be found anywhere in Europe, but often close to the coast or on rivers. Working as a BOAT BUILDER mostly requires an apprenticeship, but can be also trained on the job.
Task #1	Designing, planing and constructing boats.
Task #2	Repair and maintenance of boats
Task #3	Customer service and advice.
