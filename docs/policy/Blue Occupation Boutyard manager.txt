Boatyard manager

Property	Value
Occupation	Boatyard manager
Description of the occupation	The occupation of a BOATYARD MANAGER is to organise a boatyard where boats can be stored. This occupation can be found mostly in marinas or ports, but also as winterstorage further inland from the coast or a river. Working as a BOATYARD MANAGER requires no formal qualification, but good organisational skills and knowledge of boats and engines are of advantage.
Task #1	Organising how the boatyard will be used, where boats are stored and liasing with the boat lift to take boats out of the water or put them back in.
Task #2	Managing staff to clean the boathulls, apply antifouling, and conduct mainenance and repair work.
Task #3	Connecting to the customers/ boat owners to coordinate their wishes, make quotes and control payments and finances.
