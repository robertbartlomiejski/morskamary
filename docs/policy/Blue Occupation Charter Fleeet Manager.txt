Occupation	Charter Fleet manager
Description of the occupation	The occupation of a CHARTER FLEET MANAGER is to sell charters to customers, manage the boats and the staff.This occupation is mostly found in Marinas and Ports along the coast, oalong rivers or at lakes. Working as a CHARTER FLEET MANAGER requires no qualification, but a skipper qualification, mechanical skills and languages would be of advantage.
Task #1	Taking bookings for boat charters
Task #2	Managing charter licences, boat maintenance and repairs
Task #3	Organising staff: skippers, cleaners, maintenance
