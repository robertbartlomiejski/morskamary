Property	Value
Occupation	Crew trainer
Description of the occupation	The occupation of a CREW TRAINER is to train young people to work on yachts as crew to the captain or skipper. This occupation can be mostly found near Marinas or Ports. Working as a CREW TRAINER requires a formal qualification in line with STCW requirements in the country and of the flag state of the boat. It offers mobiity options to other sectors of Maritime Tansport, working for example as a trainer of deck hands on ships.
Task #1	Teaching theory lessons for crews including First Aid and Personal Safetyx and Social responsibilities.
Task #2	Teaching Practical lessons including Fire Fighting and Personal Survival techniques.
Task #3	Preparation and training for nautical certificates.
